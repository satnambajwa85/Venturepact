How it works?
Vetted Top Talent

We short list

You work full time with highly competitive salaries. You enjoy similar benefits and have the same growth prospects as anyone else working onsite.

You select

You interview the short listed candidates to see who fits your tech requirements and company culture. Those accepted can start full time work in a matter of weeks.
Risk Free

Trial period

During the first 2 weeks, you assess the team's contribution as they get acquainted with the codebase. At the end of the trial, you can either continue or pull the plug- no strings attached.

Negligible time investment

Our developers can learn independently and our on-boarding process is optimized to ensure that it doesnt take more than 20 mins of your CTO's time everyday. No reason not to try!
Culturally integrated

On Site work

To help build a uniform culture across both offices and to coordinate on more complicated tasks, the team leaders can travel back and forth as required- especially in the initial stages.

Co-located

The remote team itself can be co-located at one of our partner co-working spaces. Once it out grows the space, we will relocate them to a bigger space.
Optimized coordination

Asana, Skype, Github..

The developers are all trained in the use of task management systems such that the coordination of tasks happens seamlessly. They have the ability to integrate into your schedule, so you can say bye to those midnight calls!

Payments, security and legal

You don't have to worry about labor regulatory concerns and compensation. VenturePact handles salary payments to remote employees and manages their benefits. We take a 10% cut of the monthly compensation.
