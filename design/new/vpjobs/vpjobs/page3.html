How it works?

1.

Request an invite

It takes 10 minutes! Tell us about yourself and your preferred startups. We might even send you some fun coding tasks to access skills and fit.
2.

Verified by VenturePact

If everything looks good, we offer you an invite and share your resume with your preferred startups.
3.

One on One

You meet with your preferred startups and may or may not undertake a small 2-3 day task to further access fit. If all goes well, you can start right away!
4.

Work risk free

The first two weeks is 'risk free'. Either party can pull the plug if something doesn't work just right. If this happens, you still get paid pro rata and move on to your other top startup!
