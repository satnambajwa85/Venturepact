We exisit to make the world more flat.

We want to enable companies and organizations hire the best talent, no matter where it is located. To that effect, we are building a marketplace for full time and vetted remote talent.

As an employer, restricting your hiring to a small geographic region means you’re not getting the best people you can. As an employee, restricting your job search to companies within a reasonable commute means you’re not working for the best company you can. Our marketplace challenges this paradigm and connects companies to top talent worldwide.

Outsourcing is terribly broken. We want to move beyond the concept of the 'freelancer' and offer a new way for cross border collaboration! While working full time, the incentives are alligned and one can see growth opportunities very clearly!
